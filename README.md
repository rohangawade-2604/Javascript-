## Which are the Various Operators in Javascript ?

    1. Arthemetic Operator:-  + , - , * , /

        a. Modulus                   ----->     %
        b. Exponentiations           ----->     **
        c. Increment                 ----->     a++ , ++a
        d. Decrement                 ----->     a-- , --a

    2. Assignment Operator :- = , += , -= , *= , %= , **=
    
    3. Comparison Operators :- 
        
        a. Equal to                  ----->      ==
        b. Not equal to              ----->      !=
        c. Equal to & type           ----->      ===
        d. Not Equal to & type       ----->      !===

    4. Logical Operators :- 

        a. Logical And               ----->      &&
        b. Logical OR                ----->      ||
        c. Logical NOT               ----->      !
